ðŸ”¹ Bird Species Classification using CNN: VGG16 vs ResNet-152

This project compares the performance of two deep learning architectures, VGG16 and ResNet-152, in classifying bird species. The dataset consists of over 100 bird species, and the models were trained and fine-tuned using techniques like data augmentation, transfer learning, and optimization of hyperparameters.
